Lions

Lions are large, powerful carnivores found mostly in the grasslands and savannas of Africa. Known as the "King of the Jungle," lions live in social groups called prides and are the only big cats that live and hunt together. Their roar can be heard up to five miles away, and they use it to communicate with other lions and protect their territory.

What Do Lions Eat?

  • Zebras
  • Wildebeest
  • Buffalo
  • Antelope
  • Occasionally smaller animals

Fun Facts About Lions

  1. Males can weigh over 400 pounds.
  2. Lions rest for around 20 hours a day!
  3. A group of lions is called a "pride."
  4. Only the males have manes.
  5. Lionesses do most of the hunting.
